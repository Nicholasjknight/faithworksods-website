Work outside our scope

Florida's Underground Utility & Excavation contractor license covers sanitary sewer, water distribution, storm sewer systems, utility line work, directional drilling, and similar infrastructure — not the outdoor property clearing, mulching, and cleanup services Faith Works focuses on.

  • Installing underground utilities
  • Stormwater systems
  • Sewer systems
  • Water mains
  • Utility trenching
  • Site development
  • Engineered drainage systems
  • Pool installation or licensed pool contracting (we support dig-out cleanup under your pool builder)
  • Excavation contractor / utility infrastructure work
